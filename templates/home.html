Introduction

Rainfall prediction is a critical aspect of weather forecasting that plays a vital role in various sectors, including agriculture, transportation, and disaster management. Accurate predictions can help farmers optimize planting schedules, assist city planners in managing stormwater, and enable communities to prepare for potential flooding.
Traditional weather forecasting methods often rely on numerical models and historical data, which can sometimes be insufficient for real-time predictions. With the advancement of technology, there is an opportunity to enhance these predictions using image processing and machine learning. By analyzing satellite images and other weather-related visuals, we can extract valuable information about cloud formations, humidity, and other atmospheric conditions that precede rainfall.

About The Project

The outcomes of this project can significantly benefit various sectors. For example, farmers can leverage accurate forecasts to optimize their agricultural practices, while urban planners can manage water resources more effectively. Additionally, timely predictions can help communities prepare for potential flooding, enhancing overall safety and preparedness. By combining image processing and machine learning, this project aspires to create a reliable and efficient tool for rainfall prediction, contributing to better decision-making and resource management in response to changing weather patterns.
